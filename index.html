Welcome to GRAPHITE, a multimodal plotting tool that combines natural speech input with simple mouse and keyboard interaction.

You can use your mouse to enter and select points, and use the speech commands on the right as well to play with your data. In addition, you can chain together multiple commands with one mouse click by simply separating them with "and". For example,

add two comma three and fit linear

  • add <x> comma <y>
  • delete all|selected
  • zoom in|out
  • find min|max
  • invert
  • color red|orange|yellow|green|blue|purple
  • fit linear|off
  • undo
